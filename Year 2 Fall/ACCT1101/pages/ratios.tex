Compare ratios / rate of change across different companies / history to intepretate the financial health of the company.

        \hline
        Receivable Turnover               & Net Sales : Receivables                            & Efficiency in collecting receivables.                                                                                \\
        \hline
        Inventory Turnover                & COGS : Inventory                                   & Efficiency in managing inventory.                                                                                    \\
